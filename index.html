Summary

Pritory is an open source, price tracking application for small businesses. Pritory aims to help small business owners to keep an organized and up-to-date overview of their local, regional and possibly international market for their products.

Pritory is geared towards the Greek market, featuring a build-in ability to compare product prices against Skroutz API, the biggest price comparison engine in Greece.
